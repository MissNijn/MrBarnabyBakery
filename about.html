Mr Barnaby

Hi! My name is Mr Barnaby and I lived in an orphanage. I was glad when Marijke adopted me and gave me a home again. Now I feel loved, I want to be there for anyone else that every now and then feels lonely. I am always there for you to give you a hug, for a good conversation or just to sit in silence. You probably wouldn't have guessed, but I absolutely love cakes! Couldn't have been more proud that this bakery is named after me and I get the honor to join when the cake is deliverd. Seeing the smiles on people faces when they get a homebaked cake is heart-warming. And even more lucky, I generally get to taste a bit of the cake too!

Marijke

Hello! My name is Marijke and I am a PhD researcher at the University of Hull. Next to studying how clay behaves along coastlines, I have a passion for baking. I was lucky enough to work at a company with amazing colleagues, which were willing to try every cake I brought into the office. It was only when I left the company to study for my PhD, that I realised how much I like baking for others. Seeing the joy on people's faces when you bring in a homebaked cake is truly magical. As I no longer have this supportive group of colleagues around me willing to eat cake every day, I wanted to start baking for the community. For people in the area that might not have the time or the resources to bake a cake at home. But mostly to bake to make people smile.
