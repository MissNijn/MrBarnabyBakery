Welcome to the website of Mr Barnaby's Bakery. This is a small bakery for homemade bakes for special occasions, such as birthday parties, anniversaries or just for something extra with the coffee on Sunday. In the section bakes you find a collection of bakes that we generally provide. If you have a specific cake in mind, then please contact us and we will see if we can make that one for you. Please be aware that you have to order the cakes in time to give us time to prepare for it. We generally cover local delivery around Hull, but you are always welcome to pick up the cake yourself. Hope you enjoy the website!
